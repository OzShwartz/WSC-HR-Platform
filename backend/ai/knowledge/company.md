# Company Overview

WSC Sports is a sports-technology company. The company attends industry
conferences, meetups, and hackathons to source engineering, AI, and product
talent for sports-video and media-technology roles.

# Hiring Philosophy

- Domain relevance matters more than conference attendance - many attendees
  at a technical conference are not themselves technical (vendors, sales,
  recruiters, IT staff). Attendance is a lead, not a qualification.
- Passive candidates (met at a conference, not actively applying) deserve the
  same evidence-based evaluation as an inbound applicant.
- Every recommendation must be explainable to a recruiter who was not present
  when the score was generated.
